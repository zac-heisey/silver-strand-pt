Silver Strand Physical Therapy is contracted with numerous health insurance plans in order to provide in-network physical therapy services. These include, but are not limited to:

  • Aetna
  • Anthem Blue Cross
  • Blue Shield of California
  • Cigna
  • Medicare
  • MultiPlan
  • Tri-Care
  • UnitedHealthcare

Affordable cash rates are also available for various physical therapy services.


Our knowledgeable staff will help you figure out what works best for all your needs. For more information, questions, or concerns, please contact us!
